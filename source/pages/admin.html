Join Our Admin Team

Are you passionate about contributing to The Bible Channel? Follow the steps below to join our dedicated administration team and help us manage our growing community.

Application Submission

Begin by filling out the Google Form with your details and reasons for wanting to join our admin team. Once submitted, you will receive a confirmation message acknowledging receipt of your application.

Application Review

Our team will carefully review your application to assess your fit for the role. If shortlisted, you will be contacted with further instructions on the next steps.

Trial Period

You will be added to "The Bible Community" in the "Administrator Chat," where you will continuously submit your posts for review. During this phase, feedback will be provided to help you improve your contributions.

Final Evaluation

Your posts will be monitored for consistency and quality throughout the trial period. Based on your performance, you will either be offered a spot as a main channel administrator or receive constructive feedback if not selected.

Becoming an Admin

If successful, you will be welcomed as an official admin with access to the main channel. Final guidelines for your role will be provided to help you succeed in your new position.
