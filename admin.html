To become a part of the administration team, there are several steps to follow. Application Submission: Begin by filling out the Google Form with your details and reasons for wanting to join our admin team. After submitting your application, you will receive a confirmation message acknowledging receipt of your application. Application Review: Our team will review your application to assess your fit for the role. If you are shortlisted, you will be contacted with further instructions on the next steps. Trial Period: You will be added to "The Bible Community" in the "Administrator Chat," where you will continuously submit your posts for review. It's essential to follow our guidelines and submit your content for evaluation. During this phase, you will receive feedback to help you improve. Final Evaluation: Throughout the trial period, your posts will be monitored for consistency and quality. Based on your performance, you will either be offered a spot as a main channel administrator or receive feedback if not selected. Becoming an Admin: If successful, you will be welcomed as an official admin with access to the main channel and provided with the final guidelines for your role.
